# lms_system

What exact problem are you solving?
Ans: Many trainees do not know how to create a professional and job-ready CV/resume. Their resumes often miss important sections such as skills, projects, experience, and proper formatting. They also do not receive timely feedback on how to improve it.
Where does it happen in the trainee journey?
Ans:This problem happens during the later stage of the trainee journey when students prepare for internships, placements, or job applications after completing training.
Why does it matter?
Ans:A weak resume can prevent capable trainees from getting shortlisted for interviews, even if they have good skills. Without guidance and progress tracking, trainees may miss career opportunities and mentors cannot easily identify who needs help.
